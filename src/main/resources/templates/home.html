What is Contact Pro?

Contact Pro is a powerful Web Application designed to help you manage your contacts with ease and efficiency. Our application allows you to create, edit, and delete contacts effortlessly. You can also mark your favorite contacts and access them quickly. Our user-friendly interface ensures a seamless experience on both desktop and mobile devices.

This project is created by Kushwaha Madhuranjan using SpringBoot for Backend and Thymeleaf for Front End.
